Design Principles Used

Repetition: Unified the designs by repeating logo placement, brand colors, and the Museo typeface.

Contrast: Ensured readability by using light text on dark backgrounds and vice versa.

Alignment: Maintained a clean layout by aligning all elements neatly to grids.

Simplicity: Avoided clutter by focusing on essential information and design elements.
