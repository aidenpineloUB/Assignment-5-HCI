My name is Aiden Pinelo, and I come from the serene city of Belmopan. I have a strong appreciation for creativity and self-expression. Drawing and fishing are some of my favorite pastimes, offering me a sense of calm and focus. Fishing, in particular, has taught me patience and provides a meditative escape from daily life.

This past summer, I worked as a bartender, which was a transformative experience. It challenged me to step out of my comfort zone and enhanced my ability to engage with others confidently. In addition to my professional and personal interests, sports have always been a significant part of my life, fueling my competitive nature. Although my activity level has waned recently, my enthusiasm for sports remains vibrant.

Music production is another passion of mine; I used to share my beats on YouTube and still enjoy creating music with friends. It remains a rewarding and enjoyable activity.
